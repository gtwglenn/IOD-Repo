devsInc -- Dice Generator 

users will arrive at landing page (homepage) for Dice Generator 

- Dice selection page: User selects from three separate types of dice to spin 

    - selection types: D6, D10, and D12 

- Spin screen page: User is prompted by a graphical representation of a dice face (.png?) and a corresponding 'Spin' button 

    - 'Spin' --> user presses button to spin the dice (initialize random number generator)

- Results tab (Spin screen page): After each press of the 'Spin' button, 
    the webpage will subsequently record the result of the last spin of the dice in the 'Results' tab
